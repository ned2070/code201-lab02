A bit of biography

Hi! My name is Neill. I was born on Liverpool in 1970. I am not married and have no childre. I have lived in Liverpool and London and I am currently in Liverpool.

  • I have a work background in administration. If you think that is boring you are correct.
  • The worst job I've ever had was packing frozen food, which was astonishingly tedious and also quite painful.
  • I have some experience in programming but I've been out of it for a while.

  • I have no GCSEs. That's because I was in the last school cohort to take O levels. That's my excuse and I'm stickng to it.
  • I obtained an A at A level General Studies. Don't pretend your not impressed.
  • I have a third class degree in physics, for which I am inordinately pleased with myself.
  • I'm part of the way through a Master's degree in renewable energy, still have to submit the dissertation.
  • I also have a postgraduate certification in software technology from the nineties.

I'd like to get back into programming and eventually at a more senior level than I was previously. I might go self-employed, as I have been before, but I'm not wedded to it.
